# AiMedRes Stakeholder Alignment Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist ensures all key stakeholders are identified, engaged, and aligned before deploying AiMedRes in a healthcare environment.

---

## 1. Stakeholder Identification

### Executive Leadership
- [ ] Chief Medical Officer (CMO) or equivalent
- [ ] Chief Information Officer (CIO) or equivalent
- [ ] Chief Technology Officer (CTO) (if separate from CIO)
- [ ] Chief Compliance Officer or Privacy Officer
- [ ] Chief Financial Officer (CFO) (for budget approval)

### Clinical Stakeholders
- [ ] Department Heads (Neurology, Psychiatry, Radiology, etc.)
- [ ] Clinical Champions (physicians who will advocate for the system)
- [ ] Nursing Leadership
- [ ] Allied Health Professionals (as applicable)
- [ ] Clinical Informatics Team

### IT & Technical Teams
- [ ] IT Director/Manager
- [ ] Infrastructure Team (servers, networking, storage)
- [ ] Application Support Team
- [ ] Database Administrators
- [ ] Security Team / CISO
- [ ] Help Desk / Service Desk Manager

### Compliance & Legal
- [ ] Legal Counsel (contracts, liability, regulatory)
- [ ] Compliance Officer (HIPAA, GDPR, local regulations)
- [ ] Privacy Officer / Data Protection Officer (DPO)
- [ ] Quality Assurance / Clinical Safety Officer
- [ ] IRB Representative (if research or clinical trials involved)

### Operations & Support
- [ ] Project Manager / Implementation Lead
- [ ] Training Coordinator
- [ ] Change Management Lead
- [ ] Vendor Management (if applicable)
- [ ] Facility/Operations Manager (for space, power, cooling)

---

## 2. Stakeholder Engagement Activities

### Initial Briefing
- [ ] Project overview presentation delivered to executive leadership
- [ ] Clinical use case demonstrations scheduled with clinical stakeholders
- [ ] Technical architecture review completed with IT teams
- [ ] Compliance and security review scheduled with legal/compliance teams
- [ ] Budget and resource allocation approved by finance

### Requirements Gathering
- [ ] Clinical workflow requirements documented
- [ ] Technical infrastructure requirements documented
- [ ] Integration requirements with EMR/EHR systems defined
- [ ] Security and compliance requirements documented
- [ ] Training and support requirements identified

### Project Planning
- [ ] Project charter drafted and approved
- [ ] Project timeline and milestones agreed upon
- [ ] Resource allocation confirmed (staff, budget, infrastructure)
- [ ] Risk register created and reviewed
- [ ] Communication plan established

## 4. Alignment on Objectives & Success Criteria

### Clinical Objectives
- [ ] Primary clinical use cases defined and agreed upon
- [ ] Expected clinical outcomes documented
- [ ] Success metrics for clinical utility defined
- [ ] Acceptance criteria for go-live established

### Technical Objectives
- [ ] Performance targets defined (response time, uptime, throughput)
- [ ] Integration requirements documented
- [ ] Data migration/loading plan agreed upon
- [ ] Disaster recovery and business continuity requirements defined

### Compliance & Security Objectives
- [ ] Regulatory compliance requirements documented (HIPAA, FDA, etc.)
- [ ] Security controls and audit requirements agreed upon
- [ ] Privacy impact assessment completed
- [ ] Risk mitigation strategies approved

### Operational Objectives
- [ ] Support model defined (hours, escalation, SLAs)
- [ ] Training plan and schedule agreed upon
- [ ] Change management approach documented
- [ ] Maintenance windows and update procedures agreed upon

---

## 5. Budget & Resource Approval

### Financial Commitment
- [ ] Capital budget approved (hardware, software, infrastructure)
- [ ] Operating budget approved (licenses, support, maintenance)
- [ ] Training budget allocated
- [ ] Contingency budget established

### Resource Allocation
- [ ] IT staff time allocated for implementation
- [ ] Clinical staff time allocated for validation and training
- [ ] External consultants/vendors contracted (if needed)
- [ ] Dedicated project management resources assigned

---

## 6. Communication & Change Management

### Communication Plan
- [ ] Stakeholder communication schedule established
- [ ] Regular status update meetings scheduled
- [ ] Communication channels defined (email, meetings, intranet, etc.)
- [ ] Feedback mechanisms established

### Change Management Strategy
- [ ] Change impact assessment completed
- [ ] Training plan developed and approved
- [ ] User adoption strategy defined
- [ ] Go-live readiness criteria established

### Documentation
- [ ] User documentation plan agreed upon
- [ ] Technical documentation requirements defined
- [ ] Training materials development assigned
- [ ] Knowledge transfer plan established

---

## 7. Risk Management & Mitigation

### Identified Risks
- [ ] Technical risks documented (integration, performance, security)
- [ ] Clinical risks documented (workflow disruption, user acceptance)
- [ ] Compliance risks documented (regulatory, privacy, audit)
- [ ] Operational risks documented (support, maintenance, downtime)

### Mitigation Strategies
- [ ] Risk mitigation plans developed for each identified risk
- [ ] Contingency plans established for critical risks
- [ ] Risk ownership assigned
- [ ] Risk review cadence established (weekly/monthly)

---

## 8. Legal & Contractual Alignment

### Agreements & Contracts
- [ ] Business Associate Agreement (BAA) executed (if applicable)
- [ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA) executed (if GDPR applies)
- [ ] Software license agreements reviewed and approved
- [ ] Service Level Agreement (SLA) negotiated and signed
- [ ] Professional liability/indemnification clauses reviewed by legal

### Regulatory Approvals
- [ ] FDA clearance reviewed (if applicable for clinical decision support)
- [ ] IRB approval obtained (if research or clinical trials)
- [ ] State/local regulatory requirements reviewed
- [ ] Medical device classification determined (if applicable)

---

## 9. Go/No-Go Decision

### Pre-Implementation Review
- [ ] All critical stakeholders have approved proceeding
- [ ] Budget and resources are confirmed available
- [ ] Technical infrastructure is ready
- [ ] Compliance and security requirements are met
- [ ] Training and support plans are finalized

### Final Sign-Off
- [ ] Executive Sponsor approval: ___________________________  Date: _______
- [ ] Clinical Lead approval: ___________________________  Date: _______
- [ ] IT Lead approval: ___________________________  Date: _______
- [ ] Compliance Lead approval: ___________________________  Date: _______
- [ ] Project Manager approval: ___________________________  Date: _______

### Decision
- [ ] **GO** - Proceed with implementation
- [ ] **NO-GO** - Address outstanding issues before proceeding

---

## 10. Post-Alignment Activities

### Kickoff & Onboarding
- [ ] Project kickoff meeting scheduled and held
- [ ] Team onboarding and training sessions scheduled
- [ ] Regular status meetings scheduled (weekly/bi-weekly)
- [ ] Issue tracking and management system established

### Ongoing Alignment
- [ ] Monthly steering committee meetings scheduled
- [ ] Quarterly executive reviews scheduled
- [ ] Feedback loops established with clinical users
- [ ] Continuous improvement process defined
