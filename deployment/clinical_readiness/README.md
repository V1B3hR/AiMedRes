## Quick Start

### For Training Coordinators

1. Review the **clinical_operational_readiness_guide.md**
2. Customize training materials for your institution
3. Schedule onboarding sessions:
   - Clinical Staff Orientation (2 hours)
   - IT Staff Technical Training (4 hours)
   - Compliance Officer Training (1 hour)
4. Conduct training sessions
5. Assess competency
6. Provide ongoing support

### For Clinicians

1. Attend Clinical Staff Orientation session
2. Review Clinician User Manual
3. Complete hands-on exercises
4. Pass competency assessment
5. Reference Quick Start Guide for daily use
6. Contact Clinical Support with questions

### For IT Staff

1. Attend IT Staff Technical Training
2. Review System Administration Guide
3. Complete technical labs
4. Pass technical certification
5. Set up monitoring and alerts
6. Join on-call rotation

### For Compliance Officers

1. Attend Compliance Officer Training
2. Review compliance documentation
3. Understand audit procedures
4. Verify privacy controls
5. Establish incident response procedures

## Training Programs

### Clinical Staff Orientation (2 hours)
- **Audience:** Physicians, Nurses, Clinical Staff
- **Topics:** AI basics, system navigation, clinical workflows, result interpretation
- **Format:** Presentation + hands-on practice
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Certification:** Competency assessment required

### IT Staff Technical Training (4 hours)
- **Audience:** System Administrators, IT Support
- **Topics:** Architecture, configuration, monitoring, troubleshooting, backup/restore
- **Format:** Presentation + hands-on labs
- **Prerequisites:** Basic Linux/Docker knowledge
- **Certification:** Technical competency test required

### Compliance Officer Training (1 hour)
- **Audience:** Compliance Officers, Privacy Officers, Auditors
- **Topics:** Regulatory compliance, audit logging, privacy controls
- **Format:** Presentation + demo
- **Prerequisites:** Understanding of HIPAA/regulatory requirements
- **Certification:** Not required
