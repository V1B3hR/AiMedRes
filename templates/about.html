About AiMedRes

AiMedRes (AI Medical Research Assistant) is an experimental research platform for advancing AI-driven discovery and decision support in neurological and mental health conditions.

Research Focus Areas

  • Alzheimer's Disease - Early detection and progression modeling
  • Stroke and Cerebrovascular Conditions - Risk stratification
  • Parkinson's Disease and ALS - Dataset integration
  • Neurocognitive Disorders - Broader spectrum research
  • Mental Health State Modeling - Early research modules

Technology Stack

  • Adaptive neural network architectures
  • Multi-agent medical reasoning systems
  • Biological state and memory simulation
  • Explainable AI and auditability features
